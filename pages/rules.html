Server Standards

Strong rules keep the world competitive without letting it turn toxic.

CoinVale is meant to feel intense, rewarding, and fair. These expectations protect the economy, the community, and the long-term health of the server.

Respect

Keep chat clean

  • No hate speech, harassment, or targeted abuse.
  • Trash talk is not a free pass for toxicity.
  • Use common sense if the rule list misses a specific edge case.
Fair Play

No hacked advantages

  • No cheats, x-ray, dupes, macros, or exploit abuse.
  • Report game-breaking bugs instead of weaponizing them.
  • Automation should stay within whatever the server explicitly allows.
Economy

Protect the market

  • No scams that rely on broken mechanics or impersonation.
  • No real-money trading outside approved store systems.
  • Alt abuse for rewards, voting, or economy gain is not allowed.
Bases

Respect protected spaces

  • Follow claim, raid, and grief policies as configured by the server.
  • Do not lag out chunks with intentional machine abuse.
  • Build with the community experience in mind, not just your own gain.
PvP

Compete cleanly

  • No combat logging, safe-zone baiting, or glitch-kill setups.
  • Keep rivalries in the game instead of pushing them into harassment.
  • If a mechanic is disabled, do not bypass it with loopholes.
Staff

Listen when decisions land

  • Staff calls are made to protect the server, not win arguments.
  • Appeal respectfully if you think a ruling was incorrect.
  • Repeated bad-faith behavior may lead to stronger punishment.

Play Smart

When in doubt, choose the option that keeps CoinVale fun for everyone.
